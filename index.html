some more about me

  • I'm a digital designer based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Over the past few years, I've traveled a lot and taken various courses. Currently, I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Communication and Multimedia Design at the University of Applied Sciences in Amsterdam, where I’m also focusing on my minor in Designing for Emerging Technologies.
  • Traveling is one of my biggest passions. I'm half German and half Dutch, born in Spain, raised in the United States and having spent the last 16 years in Amsterdam, I love meeting new people and exploring new places around the world.

my design passion

  • I enjoy UX and UI design, but I especially love creating immersive environments and experiences. Growing up in Amsterdam, I would visit the Amsterdam Light Festival and enjoy all the light installations. That’s where my passion for experience design began. Telling a story through installations excites me, especially when they blend seamlessly with the city and are accessible to everyone.
    • I also really enjoy visiting museums and love using my museum year pass. I’m especially fond of immersive installations in museums like 'Frabrique des lumières'.
